A “budget" is just the amount of money you intend to spend on something at any given time. If you want something that costs $1000, but your monthly bills including controllable expenses is $1000, then you have no money to budget saving up for it. You have to budget your controllable expenses (groceries, toiletries, eating out, entertainment, new clothes, etc) in order to save that money instead.
